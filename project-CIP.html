Strengthening primary care services and access in Zhylyoi

Goal: Strengthening primary care services and access in Zhylyoi.

Objectives:

  1. To help primary care providers to increase their capacity, capability and responsibility for clinical quality improvement.
  2. To promote health and disease prevention among general public
    The project will include: (a) the refurbishing and upgrade of PHC centers; (b) training for personnel at all levels; (c) introducing population mapping and data driven decision making; (d) campaign and advocacy to improve and expand health, basic sanitation and nutrition education;

Effective rehabilitation of people with disabilities

To improve the quality of life of people with disabilities who move in wheelchairs in Atyrau region, their adaptation to society, to keep their psychological state under control, to implement their rehabilitation process quickly and effectively.

Objective No. 1 Integration of wheelchair users into society through independent living skills training;

Objective No. 2 Creating a pool of instructors of independent living skills from among the project graduates;

Objective No. 3 Public awareness of the problems of people with disabilities, psychological support and motivation of people with disabilities.

Horse is the best friend

Goal: to support people in achieving motorial, psychological freedom and help a person to adapt to new changing circumstances.

Objectives:

  • teaching children horseback riding to influence their physical, psycho-emotional and personal rehabilitation and adaptation;
  • helping to recover motor functions and lost skills by conducting regular therapeutic horseback riding sessions;
  • having an educational impact on children, introducing them to wildlife with the help of horses (to establish nature-friendly culture, mutual assistance; to explore the environment increasing knowledge about the nature of their region, to acquire new knowledge on how the most enthusiastic children can explore the environment);
  • creating the necessary conditions for children with disabilities to become more integrated in society;
  • to motivate and encourage children to live an active life;
  • helping families with children who have health limitations and impaired posture, general speech underdevelopment and psycho-emotional disorders using recreational activities.

Vocational Counseling - Conscious Choice of Profession

Goal Identification and development of each child's individual abilities to choose a future profession and to form independent life skills.

This goal includes the tasks below:

  • Vocational testing and counseling.
  • Social skills training - communication, teamwork, leadership, emotional intelligence.
  • Management skills training - time management and goal setting, problem solving
  • Life-skills training - financial and legal literacy, introduction to labor market, responsibility and discipline.
  • Information Literacy - effective learning, creative and critical thinking, information search and analysis.
  • Financial literacy - setting a financial goal and planning to achieve it, managing income and expenses, economics.
  • Legal literacy - basics of paperwork, contracts, government relations, employment and labor relations.
  • Communication skills - communication in personal and business relationships, teamwork, public speaking, presentation skills.
